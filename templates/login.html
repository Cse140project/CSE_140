Why this system?

Many students drop out silently. This tool helps schools spot those students early so they can receive support, counselling and resources before it's too late.

What you can do

  • Collect consistent student data across schools
  • Run dropout predictions in a few clicks
  • Identify high-risk groups for targeted interventions

How prediction works

The model analyzes factors like area type, gender, caste and standard to estimate dropout risk. It doesn’t replace teachers — it gives them an extra signal to focus attention where it matters most.
